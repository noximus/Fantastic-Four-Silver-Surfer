2. HOW TO ENTER: NO PURCHASE NECESSARY. You can enter this Sweepstakes via the Internet. For a chance to win the Grand Prize and First Prize portion of this Sweepstakes: Use a computer to access the Sweepstakes section of the Fantastic Four Rise of the Silver Surfer movie website at www.fantasticfourmovie.com. Completely fill out information requested on the Entry Form (“Entry Form”) and then transmit the Entry Form by clicking the “Submit”
button. Information requested on Entry Form consists of your name, address (including zip code), phone number (including area code), date of birth and e-mail address. There is a limit of one entry per person for this combined Grand Prize and First Prize portion of this Sweepstakes. To be eligible, all Internet entries for the Grand Prize and First Prize portion of this Sweepstakes must be received by 11:59p.m. (Pacific Time) on May 31, 2007. Additionally, only the first 400 entries received will be eligible for a chance to win the First Prize in a separate random drawing. Entries that are late (including delayed data transmissions), forged, illegible, mutilated, incomplete, misdirected, or otherwise not in compliance with these Official Rules will be disqualified.

For a chance to win an Instant Win Prize: Use a computer to access the Instant Win Sweepstakes section of the Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer movie website at www.fantasticfourmovie.com. Enter your email address and date of birth where requested. You will then have the opportunity to "flip" three graphical representations of coins on screen. If two of the three "flips" show the Silver Surfer, then the screen will indicate that you are an instant winner. If you are an instant
winner: You will be required to completely fill out and submit information requested on an Information Form (“Information Form”). Information requested on Information Form consists of your name, address (including zip code), phone number (including area code), date of birth and e-mail address. If you are not an instant winner: You will have the opportunity to enter again the following day, until the end date of the Instant Win portion of the Sweepstakes.

There is a limit of one entry per person each day in the Instant Win portion of the Sweepstakes. For clarity, you cannot enter more than one time per day, or enter using more than one email address. Only one Instant Win prize award allowed per person. Use of any automated systems to participate in the Sweepstakes is prohibited
and will result in disqualification. The instant win portion of the Sweepstakes will end on June 12, 2007 at 11:59 p.m. (Pacific Time). Entries that are late (including delayed data transmissions), forged, illegible, mutilated, incomplete, misdirected, or otherwise not in compliance with these Official Rules will be disqualified.

entrants to enter the Sweepstakes, or from all entrants who enter prior to May 31, 2007 at 11:59 p.m. (Pacific Time), whichever comes first. Sponsor's decisions are final in all respects.
Odds of winning depend on the number of eligible entries received. Winners of the Grand Prize and First Prize will be notified by e-mail or phone on/about June 1, 2007. If a potential winner cannot be reached after a reasonable effort has been exerted during one (1) day from the date of the Sweepstakes drawing, or if he/she is found to
be ineligible, or if he/she cannot or does not comply with these Official Rules, an alternate winner may be selected. Instant Win Prize winners will be notified immediately by way of an on-screen message. If you are an Instant Winner, you will be contacted by the Sponsor, or the prize will be sent to you at the address submitted on your Information Form.
